01
Core interaction

It's quick, we promise.

A single binary question — did you move today — is the only required interaction. This minimal commitment removes every excuse and creates a data series that, compounded over weeks, becomes deeply meaningful. You don't need to log your workout. You just need to show up for 8 seconds.

02
Depth by choice

The more you share, the more we can show.

Sleep quality, energy, mood, pain levels, and stress are available as optional follow-up questions. Each metric you add increases Momentum's ability to surface meaningful patterns. You're never penalized for doing less — the app adapts to how much you're willing to share on any given day.

03
Behavioral intelligence

You are the data.

At 14 days, Momentum begins surfacing behavioral patterns specific to you. At 30, statistically significant correlations emerge. You'll learn things about your own behavior that no coach could tell you — because no one has ever tracked this specific combination of your sleep, mood, pain, and movement until now.

04
Design philosophy

We get it — life happens.

There are no streak counters. No "you broke your streak" notifications. Momentum is built on the understanding that life interrupts exercise — and that the gap between intention and action is a behavioral puzzle to be solved, not a moral failing to be punished.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Plain English Summary

What we collect: Your daily check-in responses — movement, sleep, energy, mood, pain, and stress ratings — along with your email address at signup.

What we don't collect: We do not collect GPS location, contacts, payment information, or any data you don't actively provide through the app.

Research use: Aggregated, fully anonymized data may be used for behavioral research. Individual records are never shared. Research participation is always opt-in.

We never sell your data. No advertisers. No data brokers. No third-party marketing use.

Your rights: You can export, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at [email protected]

1. Who We Are

Momentum is a behavioral exercise science platform operated by Momentum Health Sciences LLC ("Momentum," "we," "us," or "our"). We are headquartered in the Washington, DC Metropolitan Area. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at [email protected].

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • —Account information (email address, optional display name)
  • —Daily check-in responses: movement (yes/no), and optional ratings for sleep quality, energy level, mood, pain, and stress (all on a 1–10 scale)
  • —Optional workout details you choose to log
  • —Communications you send to us (support emails, feedback)

Information collected automatically:

  • —Device type, operating system, and app version (for diagnostic purposes only)
  • —Timestamps of check-in completions (no location data)
  • —App usage analytics (screens viewed, features used) — anonymized

3. How We Use Your Information

  • —To generate your personal behavioral insights and pattern reports within the app
  • —To operate, maintain, and improve the Momentum platform
  • —To send you product updates and notifications you have opted into
  • —To conduct behavioral research using fully anonymized, aggregated data (opt-in only)
  • —To respond to support requests and communications

4. Research Data Use

Momentum is designed as a dual-purpose platform: it provides personal behavioral insights to users while contributing to population-level research on exercise adherence. If you opt into research participation:

  • —Your data is fully anonymized before any research use — no name, email, or identifier is ever included
  • —Research is conducted under IRB-compliant ethical guidelines
  • —You may withdraw research consent at any time without affecting your access to the app
  • —Research findings are never sold to third parties

Research participation is entirely separate from app use. Declining to participate has no effect on your personal insights or experience.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • —Service providers: Trusted vendors who help operate the platform (cloud hosting, analytics) under strict data processing agreements
  • —Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • —Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data protections transfer with the company

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • —Access and export all data associated with your account
  • —Correct any inaccurate information
  • —Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • —Withdraw research participation consent at any time
  • —Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only. We conduct regular security reviews and maintain incident response procedures.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personally identifiable data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated research data (which contains no personal identifiers) may be retained indefinitely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you by email and in-app notification of any material changes to this privacy policy before they take effect. Continued use of Momentum after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests: [email protected]
